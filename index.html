    The Growler is a neighborhood bar and restaurant located in downtown Danville. Conveniently located just off the Iron Horse Trail, we have plenty of parking in our adjacent lot and a dog-friendly patio out in front.

    The Growler serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner 7 days a week. Our menu features comfort pub food, artisan specialties, as well as some health-conscious options. We have 20 rotating taps of craft beer, premium tap wine, selected bottled wines, and distilled spirits. The Growler also has an extensive craft beer bottle and can selection in our "bottle shop" that can be enjoyed in-house or purchased to go.

    The Growler hosts a variety of special events in our adjoining parking lot. Join us for Growlerfest and Oktoberfest and sample craft beers from 20+ breweries while listening to live bands and enjoying great food!
    Other public events include 4th of July parade viewing parties and music concerts. Our parking lot is also available for private events such as class reunions, music events, and movie nights.

    The Growler is a family-friendly restaurant and we offer a "Little Growlers" menu to accommodate our younger customers. We also have a kids game section, designed to help entertain the young ones at the table. The Growler welcomes all youth groups and their families. Plan your next team party, Scout meeting. or school club event at The Growler. Our goal is to provide good food and good times for the entire family!

    In addition to our in-house dining, The Growler's sister-company. Simple Elegance Catering, provides personal and professional service for every occasion. From daily corporate lunches to elegant weddings, Simple Elegance does it all. We pride ourselves on our custom event proposals and ability to t every taste and budget.
